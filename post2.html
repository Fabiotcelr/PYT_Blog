The professionals who give hair care advice.

And as in this Online Hair Accessories Store, we love what these stylists achieve with our hair, and the hair care tips they know best; this article is born with the intention of honoring and highlighting the work of those who give their all when it comes to beauty and hair care.


Hairdressers, stylists, barbers or whatever you want to call them; they are the reason we smile when we leave the salon. Whether with a pair of scissors, a blow dryer or a flat iron, they always find the right way to enhance our features with special touches that boost our beauty.

A bit about the history of hairdressing

We have to say that hairdressing as a profession has been a part of human history for centuries. Just as you read it (we are not exaggerating). It all began in the 17th century when the hairdresser was a commoner who took care of the wigs that the nobility wore at that time. It was Louis IX, King of France, who exalted and hierarchized his work, naming his official hairdresser as "free man" thanks to his good work; giving him prestige and higher status in society.

How is it celebrated in Colombia?

Originally the date August 25 was chosen to celebrate Hairdresser's Day; however, nationally it is celebrated on August 28.

We do not know for sure the reason for this date, even so, we recognize the need to have decreed a date to highlight this work that has made so many men and women of the country happy.
