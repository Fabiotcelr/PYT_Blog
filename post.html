Break the routine in December with the best fashionable hairstyles


Whenever the December season arrives, the first thing that comes to mind is the vacations, the end of the year parties and, of course, Christmas. At this time it is more common to increase commitments with family, and social gatherings. No doubt you want to take advantage of these days to be more beautiful; and be fashionable and with the right hairstyles for the season. That's why at PYT we are going to tell you which are the best looks for you to be always in fashion, and in this month of December, you will attract everyone's attention, thanks to the way you dress, and your hair:

What hairstyle to wear for your Christmas dinner

Christmas dinner is one of the special dates in December, this day families get together, share a dinner, and celebrate the arrival of Christmas. This special day, why not be prepared to leave a good impression, and above all be at the level of this celebration; then of course, catch up with the ideas that we leave you here, and tutorials on our instagram page, how to achieve striking hairstyles, and hairstyles for hair. We start by mentioning that natural hairstyles, wavy hair, the combination of wavy style with braids, and other hairstyles such as high ponytails, are in trend, and will also help you look stylish, so learn with us, how to achieve these hairstyles so you can be more trendy without having to go to the salon.

Learn how to style your hair with PYT's hairstylists

Frizzy hair has become a trend for short hair this season because it gives a tousled feel that looks beautiful and gives a different and unusual look that will look great for Christmas and New Year's Eve. Keep in mind that short hair is perfect to wear accessories such as a headband, headdresses or clips to complement your outfit, you will be the star of the night and PYT will always be by your side to bring out the best version of you.

These are the 3 hairstyles that will be trendy in December

These are our suggestions for surviving the December season, whether you want to style your own hair, or if you have to solve the issue of styling your daughters; whether they have straight hair, or you want updos, because these days there will be different Christmas events, and meetings with friends and family, no doubt you want to leave a good impression, and be more beautiful than usual.

Unstructured ponytails

High ponytail, low ponytail, these are one of the hairstyles that are currently in trend for hair fashion, and we recommend you to wear in December.

Wavy hairstyle combined with braids

It is one of the looks of loose hair what more striking are currently, you create some waves for your hair, on one side, but at the same time, you can make one or two braids in one area of the hair, so you have a combined hairstyle wavy style, with braid, no doubt you will look according to the situation.

Low updo hairstyles

Undoubtedly an updo as can be a low tousled bun, which stylizes the neck and back; it is very versatile, and can also help you shine for your beauty, and glamor; however in this case it is more advisable for when you wear a dress with back and neckline more exposed, you will have the perfect appearance in these festivities.
